lasagna

Description

layers of broad, flat pasta (lasagna noodles) alternating with fillings like a rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses, all baked to bubbly perfection.

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • ground beef or sausage
  • tomato sauce
  • ricotta cheese
  • mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • eggs, and seasonings like basil, garlic, and salt and pepper
  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
